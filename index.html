Why Online Privacy Matters

Protect Personal Information

Your personal information is valuable. Keeping it private prevents identity theft, financial fraud, and unwanted exposure.

Professional Reputation

What's online about you affects your career. Maintaining privacy helps control your professional image.

Prevent Harassment

Privacy protects you from unwanted contact, cyberbullying, and online harassment.

Financial Security

Protecting your data helps secure your financial accounts and prevents unauthorized transactions.
